Site Plan

Site Name

Argentinian Mate & Products Online Store - This name reflects the purpose of the website, which is to provide authentic Argentinian products, especially yerba mate, to customers in the USA.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to offer a variety of authentic Argentinian products, including yerba mate, mate gourds, bombillas, and other traditional food items. Customers can browse products, learn about Argentinian culture, and make secure online purchases.

Scenarios

  • Where can I buy authentic Argentinian yerba mate in the USA?
  • What are the different types of mate gourds and which one should I choose?

Color Scheme

  • Primary Color: #75AADB (Sky Blue)
  • Accent Color: #5b8dbf (Darker Blue)
  • Background Color: #FFFFFF (White)
  • Secondary Background: #f8f8f8 (Light Gray)
  • Text Color: #333333 (Dark Gray)

Typography

The site uses the Arial, sans-serif font family for a clean, modern, and readable look. This font is applied site-wide including headers, buttons, and paragraphs.
